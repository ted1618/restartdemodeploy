Days You Will Remember

Learning from iconic companies, meeting entrepreneurs and innovators, forging friendships in late working nights, you will discover how much you can achieve in the right environment surround by like-minded people. Everyday, a new adventure.
Our campus, the Cambridge Innovation Center, hosts over 100 tech companies and Venture Cafe, the best place in the region to grow your network and be inspired.

Our Values

Working together is what makes common people achieve uncommon results. Restart One students must embrace diversity and understand that our differences make us stronger. Utmost importance is given to honesty and integrity, we demand of our people to become role models for others to follow in our community and world through their personal behavior and actions.

Our Campus

Yes, there is a classroom with a classic whiteboard and a projector. But that's as far as it gets with school looks at Restart One. From day one students are immersed in a vibrant environment, meet companies and professionals who work to push the status quo through pioneering innovations, and team up to solve problems through technology. While learning programming may happen in the classroom, exploring the world further is an integral part of every day in the program, and we believe there is no better environment than the Campus.

People

We had students who ended up working for companies they met while waiting at the coffee machine, and Restart One attracted many of its partners the same way. A new friend, career opportunity, or breakthrough idea may wait just outside the doorstep.
