This School Has No Teachers

"What if you could learn tennis from Roger Federer?" -- a simple question sparked the idea of the crowdsourced school.

Restart One is a place where students are guided not taught, each student takes ownership over their development. We do not learn from teachers, we learn from those who do - men and women who push the status quo every day through their work. In past classes we were fortunate to learn from:

Learn From Your Heroes

At Restart One we believe you are the average of the five people you spend most of your time with. Every class we put our students face-to-face with an outstanding line-up of engineers, entrepreneurs, and technologists with one thing in common: a contagious passion for what they do.

  • Powered by the Crowd

    Restart Network, through the One program, fundamentally puts together people and resources to democratize access to world-class education and equal opportunity in our community. We believe that no matter where you come from or how much money you have, you can become great if you're willing to work hard.

  • Education for our Century

    Restart One is a movement that turns most assumptions of formal education upside down. We work to create the best technology school in the world, and keep it open to people that life put in unfair circumstances. We look for grit and passion in people, check the admission page to learn more.

  • Started by Geeks

    Our founders met in college, they were both passionate about technology and shared the same disappointment with the outdated formal education system. Restart Network is run both by well accomplished individuals who want to give back and fresh graduates who want to start-out a career in tech while leaving a dent in the world.

  • Nonprofit at Heart

    Restart Network is a registered foundation in the Netherlands. We finance the program from generous donations of tech companies and individuals in our community, crowdfunding, and in-kind donations - even our campus is provided as an in-kind donation from people who believe in a world with less barriers.
